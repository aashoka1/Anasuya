About

Peerbuds is an emerging Edtech company. It seeks to develop its own platform where Engineers can meet online , raise doubts , and learn from each other (Like StackOverflow) While doing so they are looking for street-smart people who can design and implement the platform with futuristic and robust capabilities that will enable people in a community talk, interact, help, learn and collaborate things. With a recent survey on the trending technologies they have decided to use AngularJS for their front end framework.
